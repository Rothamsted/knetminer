Human Disease

Human Disease Release Notes

The final Human Disease knowledge network contains:

    Datasets used for integration

    • Gene-Protein [Ensembl]
      • A subset of 33 genes associated with Alzheimer, Parkinson and Huntington diseases was used.
      • Homo_sapiens.GRCh38.87.gff3
      • Homo_sapiens.GRCh38.pep.all.fa
      • Mapping File
      • Uniprot-Ensembl Protein Links
      • Gene Names
    • Disease [OMIM]
      • Alzheimer, Parkinson and Huntington diseases
    • Drug-Target Interactions [DrugBank]
      • DrugBank
    • Protein-Protein Interactions [IntAct]
      • Alzheimer's Disease
      • Parkinson's Disease
    • Protein-Publication [UniprotKB]
      • UniprotKB
      • Downloaded proteins involved in disease using MIM identifiers.
    • Gene Ontology
      • GO
    • Gene-SNP-Phenotype [NHGRI-EBI]
      • Data was downloaded from NHGRI-EBI GWAS Catalog
    • Homology [Ensembl]
      • BioMart Homologs Human-Mouse
      • BioMart Mouse-Uniprot
      • Downloaded Mouse Homolog proteins from UniprotKB
    • Expression [EBI-GXA]
      • Data was dowloaded from Gene Expression Atlas
      • E-MTAB-3385 experiment (The FANTOM5 Project)
      • Differential Expression
